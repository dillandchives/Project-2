Socialization

Socialization at Posh Paws focuses on being safe and low-stress for cats while building essential social skills. Please contact to request information on suitable temperaments and compatibilities. Behavioral training at Posh Paws is strongly recommended prior to daycare socialization.

Enrichment

Enrichment includes stimulating playtime activities and leashed outdoor excursions. A high definiton web cam feed is available for owners to be easily updated on their beloved pets' activities. Leash training is offered at Posh Paws as part of our training program and recommended prior to daycare enrichment.

Boarding

Overnight boarding is an option for cats who require a longer stay. Our boarding section includes spacious individual areas for each cat with access to windowed spots. In addition to these private compartments, we have a common area with stimulating activities that is accessible for cats who are comfortable with and prepared for a social boarding experience.
