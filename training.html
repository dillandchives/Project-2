Our training services

Training at Posh Paws promotes cat stimulation and enrichment through the use of effective research-based techniques, patience, and positive reinforcement. Benefits include a sharper more alert mind, well-rounded temperaments and behaviors, and increased opportunities for feline-human bonding.

Beginner training introduces basic behavioral training through positive reinforcement and clicker use.
Fundamental commands are taught and practiced thoroughly.

Intermediate training builds on basic behavioral training and transitions to using vocal and gestural signals.
New commands are added and are sequenced into short combinations.

Advanced training develops sophisticated social temperaments around humans and cats.
Cats will develop the ability to follow a sequence of increasingly complicated commands.

Service Details

  • Development of positive behaviors
  • Development of socializing skills
  • At-home exercises
  • Basic clicker training
  • Transition to vocal and gestural commands
  • Commands sequences ranging from one-word to a sequence
